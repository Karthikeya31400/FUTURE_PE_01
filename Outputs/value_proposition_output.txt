ORIGINAL HEADLINE:
"A Heritage-Tech app that scans artifacts and translates ancient languages."

IMPROVED VERSION (Primary Option):
"Turn your device into a digital portal to the ancient world."

ALTERNATIVE OPTIONS:

"Scan history, translate the lost, and lead your own expedition."

"Breathe life into silent relics with AI-powered interactive discovery."

"Stop reading history—step into it with the MythiLens portal."

IMPROVEMENT LOGIC:

Clarity: Shifted from a functional description ("scans and translates") to an outcome-driven metaphor ("digital portal"). This immediately tells the user what they become (an explorer) rather than just what the app does.

Benefit Enhancement: The word "Portal" implies immersion and discovery, which are the primary emotional drivers for history enthusiasts.

Specificity: By removing the clunky "Heritage-Tech" label from the headline and focusing on the "Ancient World," the value proposition becomes tangible and exciting for a broader audience while staying under the 10-word mark.
